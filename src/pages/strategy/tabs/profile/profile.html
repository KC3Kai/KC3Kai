The page is empty and an error message is on the top of page.
You will need to play the game with the devtools "KanColle" panel opened. Use the gold heart extension icon on your Chrome browser's top right corner and select the option to play Kantai Collection. Activate the devtools panel with F12 or Ctrl+Shift+J.
With the devtools open, find and select the "Kancolle" tab from the top menu of the devtools. You may need to press the ">>" in order to find it. Remember to NOT let the game start until this panel has been activated. Once you're logged in to the DMM website, the game will proceed to start automatically.
This is the only way for KC3 to be able to detect your in-game data. Once the game master data is acquired, all of the Strategy Room tabs will become functional.
Why are there no quest translations or subtitles in-game?
The options to turn these on are available on-screen after selecting to play Kantai Collection and BEFORE you press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+J in the instructions above.
The percentages under "Statistics" are wrong.
The in-game admiral profile screen has its own algorithm for these stats.
There's an option to clear profile data and an option to clear history. What's the difference?
Profile data is data that can be read directly from the game: ship list, equipment list, fleet composition, quests, map gauges, etc. Clearing your profile data deletes information that can be easily replaced by moving through in-game menus, particularly the main screen and the quest menu.
History is data that KC3 has recorded over the course of your time playing while the viewer was active. It isn't data that can be acquired directly through in-game menus. Clearing your history will delete your records for all accounts that have played on this browser profile and cannot be recovered without a data backup.
Does clearing data have any effect on my in-game data?
No. KC3 does not alter your in-game data in any way. Only the records and logs saved to your computer are modified by this viewer.
How do I make sure my profile data is updated within the Strategy Room?
Click on the heart logo located at the top of the sidebar to the left. You don't need to use your browser's
⟳Reload this page button unless specified to do so.
Can I export my history and profile data to another device?
Go to the Data Backup tab.
How do I export my ship and equipment list to a single image?
Go to the Showcase tab and select the Export options at the top of the page.
Why aren't the bug fixes working?
Try closing your game tab first. Some data modifications require the game to be closed in order to avoid conflicts.
