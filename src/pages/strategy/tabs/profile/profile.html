The page is empty (and an error message on the top of page)
You will need to play the game with the Admiral "F12" Panel open. Remember that DO NOT let game start before "KanColle" tab panel has been actived (In fact game will be started automatically when panel active). That is the only way we can know of your in-game data. Use the Gold Heart icon on your Chrome to start playing. Once game master data gets, all Strategy Room tabs are functional. And you may play game without panel opened next time (not recommend this anyway).
The rate percentages under statistics are wrong
Visit the in-game admiral profile screen to get the exact rates. The game has a special formula for it, not just basic percentage between the counts.
What is "Profile Data"?
It refers to data that can be sourced from the game: ship list, equipment list, fleet arrangement, quests, map gauges, etc.
What encompasses "Histories"?
It refers to logs saved onto the computer such as sortie history, resouce history, crafting history, etc. Clearing Histories will delete records for all accounts that played on this browser profile.
How will this affect my game account?
All of this does not affect your game account, only the records and logs saved by on your computer by this extension.
How to make my changed in-game "Profile Data" affect pages of this tab?
Not necessary to use "Reload Button" of your Chrome, just click the "Heart Logo" on the top left side to reload latest data at any time.
Can I export my whole history (not just profile data) and move it to another device?
Go to "Data Backup" page.
The bug fixes not working for me?
Try to close your game tab first, some data modifications require the game closed to avoid conflicts.
