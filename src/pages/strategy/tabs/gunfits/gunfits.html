What is this page?
To collect data regarding gun fits, we require players to run fit tests. This page is meant to organize the testing effort and allow testers to check which tests are currently avaliable.
How do I help out?
If you have the required ships and equipment specified below, sortie to 1-1 or 1-2 with the ship and equips specified, then retreat after the first node. Do take note of the morale range, this is morale before battle started, so at port screen or map selection.
Thank you for contributing!
Why is my player ID and username being submitted?
This is so that we can filter out data tampering.
What other data is being submitted?
Current battle conditions.
What about improved gear?
If possible, please use base equipment for testing.
What is Total Radar Accuracy / 203mm Total Accuracy Penalty requirement?
For Total Radar Accuracy, that is the required amount of accuracy stat from equipped radars you must use for this test. For 203mm Total Accuracy Penalty, that is the required amount of accuracy penalty from equipped 203mm/53 Twin Gun Mount you must use for this test. You do not have to factor in the equip accuracy of the gun(s) being tested.
