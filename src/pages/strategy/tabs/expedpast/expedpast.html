What do colors mean since 2019-07-18?
Monthly Expedition: blue name, can be succeeded once every month, reset at 12:00 JST on the 15th;
Combat Expedition: red time, your fleet may take damage from enemy.
btw, expedition name with * mark means sortie support expedition which cannot be cancelled.
Mouse and key operation trivia:

Holding Alt key and clicking on a checkbox of expedition or fleet, will check the selected item only instead of toggling it.

Clicking on any ship icon of result list will show the fleet in Fleet Manager. Holding Ctrl or Meta key will open Fleet Manager in new tab.
