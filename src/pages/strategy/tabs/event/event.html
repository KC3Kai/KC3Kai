About REPLAY FEATURE

Left click on a REP button to download sortie card images. You may watch it at the REPLAY WEBSITE https://bit.ly/kc3-replayer. You can also share this to your friends so they can watch it too. Be careful though, do not upload directly to Facebook or other websites that resize or change the quality of the picture. Try using imgur, puush, Twitter, etc.

Right clicking on the REP button will automatically open the REPLAY WEBSITE with your replay.

Hold Alt key and left click on the REP button will just copy your replay data (JSON text) to clipboard.

Show Fleet Manager

Expand Fleet section by clicking on F button,
a To Fleet Manager button will be found to show the fleets in Fleet Manager page.
Left click on Sortie ID next to REP button, will also do the same thing.

Hold Ctrl or Meta key and click will open Fleet Manager in new tab.
