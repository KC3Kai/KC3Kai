About REPLAY FEATURE
Left click on a REP button to download sortie card images. You may watch it at the REPLAY WEBSITE. You can also share this to your friends so they can watch it too. Be careful though, do not upload directly to Facebook or other websites that resize or change the quality of the picture. Try using imgur, puush, Twitter, etc.

Right clicking on the REP button will automatically open the REPLAY WEBSITE with your replay.

There are incoming features such as the ability to use normal in-game screenshots for replay. This is in our dev-ssdata branch in our GitHub repository, but needs further testing.
