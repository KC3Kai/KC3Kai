What does this page do?
It lets you input your expedition preferences on which resource type is your priority and AFK hours, then gives you the best three expeditions to run during that time.
Does this consider resupply costs?
Yes, it is necessary to consider resupply costs in the calculations to give you the best output. Expeditions that are actually worse may get ranked better just because resupply is not considered when they have much higher costs. See discussion.
Then what do you use as the minimal resupply costs?
See Javran's gist.
What do I put in the resource text boxes?
You can give each resource a priority, of any whole number. It will be considered so that the results are affected by the resource type you want most.
What is the "AFK Time"?
The hours and minutes you're goign to be offline, and is also considered in scoring the expeditions.
