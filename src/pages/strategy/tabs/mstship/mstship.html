Why are some values of battleship fit gun bonus still unknown or looking wrong?
Fit gun mechanics are still being investigated to this day, hence the values are not final and may be subject to changes. Accuracy verifications are hard work, to get a conclusion, mass samples needed to be summarized. Data source.
What do those icons mean for abyssal?
  • Boss icon with horns means its border color of icon will be rendered with boss style.
  • Ship icon means you have ever encountered this exact abyssal ship, her stats and equipment are from your history database. Otherwise, they are from KC3 internal data which are collected by us manually, that means they might be out of date or inaccuracy. Besides, equip slot capacities are always from internal data.
  • If you see different text color of stats or equipment, that means our internal data is different with your encounter's. You could help us by reporting the correct value on tool-tip to us.
  • If you see no stats and equipment but a JSON text, that means there is no data yet for her in our internal data. You could help us once you encounter her.
