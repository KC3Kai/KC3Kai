Why does "Current Fleets" not matched with the fleets in game?
Go to home port in game once, then click on Heart Logo to refresh with latest data.
Why eLoS or fighter power looks different? Gear improvement and plane proficiency disappeared?
Because these data, such as gear improvement and plane proficiency, are not saved to old sortie history record. They are not able to completely be reproduced when import from old history.
Furthermore, as your HQ level also not saved aside, value of eLoS Formula 33 might be affected by your current HQ level.
Why support power is 0 or quite few?
Because ships' fire and torpedo power with equipments are not saved to sortie history. Support power is not able to be reproduced when import from history, although some fixed powers are given to CV(L).
