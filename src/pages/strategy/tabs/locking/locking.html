How do I mark a ship for a color?
Drag her icon from the ship list at the bottom, onto designated dropping boxes for each color.
How do I remove a ship from a colored box?
Double click on her face at box, drag her out of box, or click her tag cell at list repeatedly.
I cannot remove a ship from a colored box?
You cannot removed ships already locked in game, and are signified by the icon's transparency.
When I click "Clear All", some ships remained
As I said, ships already locked in game are also locked on the colored boxes here.
The ships get re-arranged after refreshing
Do not mind the arrangements. This page's goal is to let you plan for the whole fleet's separation into different operations, not to arrange your compositions.
What does Tag column mean?
If you see number in tag column it means that ship is already locked in game. You can't do anything about game locked ships. Otherwise try clicking inside cell. It should cycle through tags of that ship.
