What do the red boxes and colored numbers mean?
Red text and equipment boxes mean you cannot execute this upgrade. It's either you do not have this item or item with these stars, or you do not have any of the required ships, insufficient resources, devmats, screws or related items to be consumed.
Yellow numbers mean the amount of items to be consumed you already have, but they are currently equipped or locked.
Why are some amounts question mark? why are some improvable items in game not here?
Database for these items are not up to date yet, please wait for the results of verifying and updating.
Alternatively visit wiki pages or utility sites like: WhoCallsTheFleet Arsenal, Simplified Chart for Akashi.
What are those green amounts in brackets?
They are amounts required to guarantee success.
Why does the prerequisite checking not matched with my data in game?
Go to home port in game once, then click on Heart Logo to refresh with latest data.
