Why do I need an External Backup?
This can be useful for external backup and sync via different devices.
How to get Access Token for Dropbox?
Go to My Apps page and click "Create app" button.
Choose "Dropbox API" in the first point.
Choose "App folder" in the second point.
Enter App name "KC3Kai" or what you want.
Click "Create app" button.
Go to "Settings" tab and click "Generate" button under "Generated access token".
