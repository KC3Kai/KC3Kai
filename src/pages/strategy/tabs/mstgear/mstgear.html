Why the names of types are in Japanese? What do those numbers mean?
Some of them are from original game master data, some of them might be still untranslated or undefined.
The 5 numbers are also type identity of game data, stand for: major category, minor category, master category, icon type, aircraft image type. The names are in reversed order of first 3 categories.
Why can not find stats of interception and anti-bomber from Interceptor Fighter?
They are reusing existed stats in oginal game master data, which hit represents the Anti-Bomber, evasion represents the Interception.
