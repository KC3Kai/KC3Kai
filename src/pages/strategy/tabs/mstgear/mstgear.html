Some rarity stars are wrong?
Rarity values are taken from game master data, used by in-game rarity text and BG image displaying instead of stars. Card images and the stars on each card might have changed between the time they made the assets and the present data, inconsistencies have been found on some early implemented items with 2 or 3 stars.
Why the names of types are in Japanese? What do those numbers mean?
Some of them are from original game master data, some of them might be still untranslated or undefined.
The 5 numbers are also type identities of game data, stand for: category type, card type, equip type, icon type, aircraft image type. The names are the first 3 types in reversed order.
Why can not find stats of interception and anti-bomber from Interceptor Fighter?
They are reusing names of existed stats in oginal game master data, which hit accuracy represents the Anti-Bomber power, evasion represents the Interception power.
