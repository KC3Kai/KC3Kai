About REPLAY FEATURE

Left click on a REPLAY button to download sortie card images. You may watch it at the REPLAY WEBSITE. You can also share this to your friends so they can watch it too. Be careful though, do not upload directly to Facebook or other websites that resize or change the quality of the picture. Try using imgur, puush, Twitter, etc.

Right clicking on the REPLAY button will automatically open the REPLAY WEBSITE with your replay.
Holding Alt key and right clicking on the REPLAY button will open the battle logs instead.

Hold Alt key and left click on the REPLAY button to just copy your replay data (JSON text) to clipboard.
